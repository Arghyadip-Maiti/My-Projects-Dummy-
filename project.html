Our Projects

The Open Source × GSoC Club thrives on impactful projects that solve real-world problems. From student-led initiatives to GSoC contributions, our members build tools that empower communities, improve workflows, and push the boundaries of open collaboration.

CodeHealth Tracker

A privacy-first health monitoring app built with open standards. Tracks medicine intake, hydration, and daily routines. Developed during our Spring Hackathon.

DevSync

A collaborative coding platform that syncs real-time edits across devices. Designed for remote teams and open source contributors. GSoC 2025 finalist project.

GreenDocs

An eco-friendly documentation generator that reduces redundant code and promotes clean architecture. Used by multiple student-led startups.

OpenVote

A decentralized voting system for campus elections. Built with blockchain APIs and audited by community contributors. Featured in our Innovation Showcase.
